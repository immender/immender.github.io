Foreword

This is a prototype for Goddess Metempsychosis, a roleplaying game written by Mender, based on the Shin Megami Tensei series. This version of the game is free to play and distribute. A paid version will be forthcoming, with all player-facing content available for free.

Goddess Metempsychosis

Goddess Metempsychosis is a pen-and-paper roleplaying game where players shape the future of an uncertain world as the balance of chaos and law crumbles.

Great Cataclysm

In one way or another, a cataclysm has entirely transformed the world as your characters know it. Whether that cataclysm is a global nuclear war, the latest in a cycle of universal rebirth, or some fantastical armageddon is left as an exercise to the players and game master.
